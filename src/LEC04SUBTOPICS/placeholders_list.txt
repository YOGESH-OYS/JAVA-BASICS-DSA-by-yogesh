Common Format Specifiers:
%d - Integer (decimal)
%o - Integer (octal)
%x or %X - Integer (hexadecimal)
%f - Floating-point number (decimal)
%e or %E - Floating-point number in scientific notation
%g or %G - Floating-point number in decimal or scientific notation (whichever is shorter)
%a or %A - Floating-point number in hexadecimal
%c - Character
%s - String
%b or %B - Boolean
%h or %H - Hashcode
%n - Platform-specific newline
